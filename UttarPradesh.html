Uttar Pradesh Itinerary

Here's a suggested itinerary for your trip to Uttar Pradesh:

  • Day 1: Arrival in Agra, check-in to hotel, visit the iconic Taj Mahal at sunset.
  • Day 2: Explore Agra Fort, visit the Tomb of Itimad-ud-Daulah, and enjoy local cuisine.
  • Day 3: Travel to Jaipur, stopping at Fatehpur Sikri en route, then check-in to hotel.
  • Day 4: Visit Amber Fort, City Palace, and Hawa Mahal, and explore the vibrant local markets.
  • Day 5: Head to Varanasi, check-in, and take an evening boat ride on the Ganges River.
  • Day 6: Witness the morning Aarti ceremony, visit Sarnath, and explore the narrow alleys of Varanasi.
  • Day 7: Return to your starting point or continue your journey.

Feel free to customize this itinerary based on your interests. Don't miss out on sampling the delectable Awadhi and Mughlai cuisine, and immerse yourself in the rich culture and history of Uttar Pradesh!
